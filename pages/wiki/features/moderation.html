Moderation Commands

Here are some of the moderation commands that can be used:

  • /moderate kick [member] [reason] - Kick a member from the server.
  • /moderate mute [member] [reason] - Mute a member.
  • /moderate ban [member] [reason] - Ban a member from the server.
  • /moderate warn [member] [reason] - Warn a member.
  • /moderate lock [channel] - Lock a channel.
  • /moderate unlock [channel] - Unlock a channel.
