Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some common questions about Wadder.

What is Wadder?

Wadder is an AI-powered Discord bot designed to help users manage their servers, engage with their community, and provide various features and utilities.

How do I invite Wadder to my Discord server?

To invite Wadder to your Discord server, follow the invite link provided on our website. You'll need to grant Wadder the necessary permissions to function correctly in your server.

What features does Wadder offer?

Wadder offers a variety of features, including server moderation, user management, entertainment features, and integrations with popular platforms like Minecraft.

How do I set up Wadder on my server?

After inviting Wadder to your server, use the provided commands to configure the bot's settings and preferences according to your needs. You can find more information on setting up Wadder in our Wiki.
